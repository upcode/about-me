Hair by Al

Created a business web page for Hair Stylist who looking to gain web presence to attrack new customers.
Project Details

  • Client: Al Millian
  • Date: April 2017
  • Online: Business Website

Development

Stack:Pyton, JavaScript / jQuery, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3, Flask, Heroku

Design

Business website as simple site for users to findinformation about Ryan's painting services.
Concept Ideation: Define
What is the purpose:

The concept of Hair by Al is to gain web presence for new clients. Also easier way for users to find information about services as well as booking appointments

Problem trying to solve:

Client’s goal is to gain new clients with web presence where users can learn about stylist and his services he offers.

Goals:

  • Web Presence
  • Way to book appointments
  • Way to contact
Notes about project
Scenario:

Looking online in your local area for professional hair stylist

  • user is on public transit
  • user checks emails
  • user clicks Democracy Tuesday
  • user has a quick read of content
  • user can choose to continue to read or click other content
user story scenario
Sketching Interactions:

Based on the concept and user scenarios, and clients goals I then sketched the interface interactions. The sketches take into account each platform for which I am designing the website: IOS, Andriod, and Web

Wireframes:

I created wireframes that detail where the elements and content should be placed on each section of the webpage.

After learning about client’s vision and needs for his website. I did additional research to learn more about stylist and salons. This allowed me to make sure the right content was added for this type of industry. After the research I created vision board for Client’s website.

Mockups: First iteration

II created a simple look with header as client did not have much content. After research and working with client to collect content I was able to create a new mockup.
