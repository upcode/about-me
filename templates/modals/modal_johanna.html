Le Mur Des Fleurs (The Wall of Flowers) wall coverings bring life and dimension to the classical floral wallcovering. This contemporary, dramatic, large panel (50" X 216") of floral fine art printing on suede paper invites a closer look at the intricate details of petals and flowers; what they become as a whole making it a truly unique experience when entering any space. A fine art photographic installation in your personal space, Le Mur Des Fleurs wall coverings forces us to examine our human obsession to look at flowers; their simplicity, complexity- their mystery, keep us in awe.

Wallpaper designs use a variety of flowers to create their unique look, including Josephine Peonies, Evelyn Roses, and Coco Dahlias. For those interested in color and a more dramatic feel in any room, Le Mur Des Fleurs now offers color too. Josephine Peonies is a popular pick of Peonies in fuchsia, or our collection of Roses: Evelyn Roses in white, Almina Roses in red, and Lucille Roses in yellow.
