World Traveler

Blogger's Travels
Project Details

  • Client: Rebecca Nguyen
  • Date: April 2017
  • Online: Blog Website

Development

Stack:Pyton, JavaScript / jQuery, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3, Flask, Heroku

Design

Bloggers website to journal her travels for new and current users.
Concept Ideation: Define
What is the purpose:

The concept of World Traveler is to gain more web presence for new users. Also easier way for users to learn about bloggers travels.

Problem trying to solve:

Client’s goal is to creative way to blog about her travels where many users can learn and experience her travels.

Goals:

  • Web Presence
  • Way to connect to new users
Scenario:

Readers reading about bloggers travel posts

  • user is on public transit
  • user checks phone
  • user clicks World Traveler
  • user has a quick read of content
  • user can choose to continue to read or click other content
Sketching Interactions:

Based on the concept and user scenarios, and clients goals I then sketched the interface interactions. The sketches take into account each platform for which I am designing the website: IOS, Andriod, and Web

Wireframes:

I created wireframes that detail where the elements and content should be placed on each section of the webpage.

After learning about client’s vision and needs for her website. I did additional research to learn more about blogging sites. This allowed me to make sure the right content was added for this type of industry. After the research I created vision board for Client’s website.

Final Mockup:

I created a simple layout highlting important content as well. I kept it simple as I wanted content to be most important peice of the site.
