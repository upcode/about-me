Hair by Al

Hair Stylist webiste

What is the purpose:

The concept of Hair by Al is to gain web presence for new clients. Also easier way for users to find information about services as well as booking appointments

Problem trying to solve:

Client’s goal is to gain new clients with web presence where users can learn about stylist and his services he offers.

Goals

  • Web Presence
  • Way to book appointments
  • Way to contact

Scenario:

Reading the news as you go to work
  • user is on public transit
  • user checks emails
  • user clicks Democracy Tuesday
  • user has a quick read of content
  • user can choose to continue to read or click other content

Sketching Interactions:

Based on the concept and user scenarios, I then sketched the interface interactions. The sketch was of three different email designs. The sketches take into account each platform for which I am designing the newsletter : IOS, Andriod, and Web.
