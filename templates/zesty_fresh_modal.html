Democracy Tuesday

Newsletter to empower users with update current news

What is the purpose:

The concept of Democracy Tuesday is to empower individuals to stay updated with current news. Finding creditable news sources is a significant issue today. We are targeting young professionals in hopes the newsletter provides a quick read to insightful news on current events and politics. The newsletter will engage users in a quick read and also allow them to take action in their own local communities.

Problem trying to solve:

Client’s goal is to reach out to as many users as possible who are interested in learning and participating in current politics. The concept is to allow them to gain insight and exposure to current topics while to staying up- to-date and informed. Current newsletter is text email where look and feel do not convey client’s excitement about user’s engagement in politics.

Goals

  • need a way to reach out to new and current users
  • need a way to interest more users with an attractive interface
  • desire a customized logo and banner

Scenario:

Reading the news as you go to work
  • user is on public transit
  • user checks emails
  • user clicks Democracy Tuesday
  • user has a quick read of content
  • user can choose to continue to read or click other content

Sketching Interactions:

Based on the concept and user scenarios, I then sketched the interface interactions. The sketch was of three different email designs. The sketches take into account each platform for which I am designing the newsletter : IOS, Andriod, and Web.
